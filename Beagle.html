Beagle

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the beagle is employed as detection dogs for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world.
